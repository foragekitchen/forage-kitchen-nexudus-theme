Forage Kitchen is a co-working space for food. Our mission is to expand the local food economy by supporting its producers. With workspace, business support, and connection to local farmers, we make the business of starting a business just a little bit easier.

Need a clean, well-equipped space to knock out your prep? Want access to top-flight stovetops and ovens to get more out of your ingredients? Need office space and a business address? Forage not only offers top-of-the-line tools, but a home for your passion.

Together with Chefs, Makers and support staff, we are building a creative community, with a shared belief that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we source only local, natural products for use in our space. Forage is a great place to start a business, work on a big project, take a class, or drop in for a meal.

We're currently in build-out mode on our new location in Oakland, but we're accepting applications for folks who want to use the space. Look over our site to see what we’re about and if Forage Kitchen is a good fit for you.  We hope to meet you soon!

Are you a Chef?

Chefs are the beating heart of Forage Kitchen. We know how hard it is to run your own shop, and we want to help. We start you off with dedicated kitchen space, but offer so much more. From office space, to a dishwasher on staff, to a personal mailbox so you’ll get your business mail where you do your business.

Are you a Maker?

Sometimes all that stands between you and your culinary dreams is access to top-flight equipment and an environment where you can be focused and inspired.

Whether it's to jar jam from your bumper crop of persimmons, or scratch that itch you've had to cure your own meat, we've got the space for you to stop dreaming and start doing. 

We help you order great local ingredients, have professionals on hand to help you cook, and we even do the dishes.
